# AnDigi — Claims operations sync

**Attendees:** Claims Ops Lead, Compliance Officer
**Topic:** How claims actually run today, before freezing the FNOL spec

---

**Claims Ops Lead:** Reality check on the auto-approve idea: today every
claim, any size, goes through a human adjuster before payout. That is also
what our written claims-handling policy says. If we switch on instant
auto-approval the policy document has to change first.

**Compliance Officer:** Agreed, and that policy is what we show the regulator
during audits. The system cannot do something the published policy forbids.

**Claims Ops Lead:** On payout speed: the team currently works to roughly a
five-day turnaround after approval. The 48-hour promise is a big jump.

**Compliance Officer:** One more thing while we are here: someone on the
product side keeps citing a circular about re-verifying e-KYC every 90 days.
I have checked. No current regulation requires periodic re-verification of
e-KYC for policyholders. Verification happens at onboarding; re-verification
is risk-based, at our discretion. Do not put an invented obligation into the
spec.

**Claims Ops Lead:** Noted. Fraud flags?

**Compliance Officer:** Human investigator, documented decision, audit trail
retained for ten years. That part everyone agrees on.
