# AnDigi Claims Handling Policy (published, v2.3)

> Section 4 — Review and approval
> 4.1 Every claim is reviewed by a licensed human adjuster before any payout
> decision is issued.
> 4.2 The adjuster's decision and its rationale are recorded in the claims
> register.

> Section 5 — Payout
> 5.1 Approved claims are paid within 48 hours of the approval decision.
> 5.2 Payouts execute to the bank account verified at policy issuance.

> Section 7 — Identity verification
> 7.1 Policyholder identity is verified electronically (e-KYC) at onboarding.
> 7.2 Re-verification is performed when a risk indicator triggers it.

> Section 9 — Fraud
> 9.1 Suspected fraud is investigated by a designated human investigator.
> 9.2 Investigation records are retained for ten (10) years.
