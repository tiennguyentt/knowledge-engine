# AnDigi — Founder direction meeting (FNOL & AI triage scope)

**Attendees:** CEO, CTO, Claims Ops Lead
**Topic:** Claims first-notice-of-loss (FNOL) and AI triage for AnDigi v1

---

**CEO:** First, the filing experience: customers file the loss in the app —
photos, a description, the incident date. No call centers.

Then the headline decision: claims under 5 million VND that pass AI triage
get auto-approved instantly. No human in that path. Speed is the product.

**Claims Ops Lead:** Including motorbike physical damage? That's most of our
volume.

**CEO:** If the triage score clears the threshold and there is no fraud flag,
yes. Instant.

**CTO:** Payout timing once approved?

**CEO:** Forty-eight hours from approval to money in the customer's account.
That's the promise we print on the policy.

**CTO:** e-KYC?

**CEO:** Customers verify identity once at onboarding. We do not ask again
unless a risk flag fires. Don't make people re-photograph their ID every
quarter, it kills conversion.

**CTO:** Fraud handling?

**CEO:** Any fraud flag routes to a human investigator, always. The AI never
clears its own fraud flag.

**Claims Ops Lead:** And claims above 5 million?

**CEO:** Human adjuster reviews, decision within 3 business days.
